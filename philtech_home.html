The Philippine Technological Institute of Science Arts and Trade Inc. (PHILTECH) was founded in 2010 in Tanay, Rizal, by a diverse group of eleven professionals, including engineers, IT experts, and educators. Starting as a non-stock, non-profit institution, it initially offered two-year technical programs in Information Technology, Hotel and Restaurant Services, and Business Outsourcing Management. In 2013, its first graduates completed their studies, supported by TESDA-Rizal accreditation, and the school expanded with new branches in Sta. Rosa, Laguna, and General Mariano Alvarez (GMA), Cavite, serving over 1,100 students by 2015-2014.

PHILTECH continued evolving to meet community needs, launching its first bachelor's program‚ÄîBachelor in Technical Vocational Teacher Education (Major in Food and Beverage Management)‚Äîat the GMA branch in 2021. By 2022-2023, it introduced additional degree programs, including Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Office Administration, attracting 193 students. The institution emphasizes quality instruction and facilities, aligning with TESDA standards and expanding its academic reach.

Committed to fostering global success through accessible education, PHILTECH prioritizes technical-vocational training, academic excellence, and skill development. As a patriotic, non-sectarian institution, it focuses on empowering Filipino youth to become productive citizens. With a dynamic faculty and a mission centered on community service, PHILTECH remains dedicated to molding future leaders while upholding its founders' vision of blending innovation, education, and social responsibility.

PhilTech Vision and Mission

Vision

Philippine Technological Institute of Science Arts and Trade, Inc. (PHILTECH) is dedicated to give quality education to the development of every Filipino. It aims to hone students who are values oriented and physically, academically, socially, and spiritually committed to the achievement of life-long learning and service to the nation.

Mission

PHILTECH shall emerge as one of the educational institutions in the Philippines that can provide academic excellence, appropriate technology and produce graduates who are globally competitive and shall serve as pillar in this challenging world.
